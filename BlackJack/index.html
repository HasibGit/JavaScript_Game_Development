BlackJack Game

Rules
  • There are in total 13 types cards.
  • Cards from 2 to 10 represent values from 2 to 10.
  • Cards Jack, Queen, King represent value 10.
  • The Ace card can represent 2 values, 1 and 11.
  • First you will make your moves, then the bot will make his moves.
  • Each player will have to make atleast one move
  • In each move cards are picked at random and score is added to the players score according to the card value.
  • Whoever has greater score wins. But if a players score crosses 21, then he is busted and if the other player is not busted , the other player will win.
  • If both player has the same score or both players are busted, it's a draw.
  • If a player gets ACE card, 11 point's will be added to players score if the players score doesn't exceed 21 by adding 11 points. Else, only 1 point will be added to the players score.
